Please join us on Saturday 22nd May 2021 at The Landmark Arts Centre, Teddington (Find us). We are proudly holding our first ever event aimed at new and aspiring allotment holders. You may have recently taken on an allotment and be feeling overwhelmed, on the waiting list for an allotment or just interested in what would be involved. Come and hear from our speakers and gain insight, knowledge and tips on what's involved during your first year as an allotment holder.

Schedule of Events - Saturday 22nd May 2021

Time What's on Find out more
09:00 - 10:00 Meet, greet and refreshments
10:00 - 10:45 Stuart Lodge - Plan, plan, plan

Find out from Stuart the advantages of thorough planning. From what to grow and where to grow it, he will offer you tips on what to consider in order to achieve nature's best rewards.
10:45 - 11:15 Q&A with Stuart Lodge
11:15 - 11:30 Short break
11:30 - 12:15 Sahera Chohan - Preparing your plot

Sahera will talk you through the hard work of preparing your plot. Good preperation leads to a more fruitful crop and she will guide you step by step with what's involved.
12:15 - 12:45 Q&A with Sahera Chohan
12:45 - 13:45 Lunch
13:45 - 14:30 David Lillywhite - Start small - don't lose the plot!

David will offer insight and tips in order to start small and build your allotment over time rather than becoming overwhelmed with an impossible challenge.
14:30 - 15:00 Q&A with David Lillywhite
15:00 - 15:15 Short break
15:15 - 16:00 Betty Tordoff - Maintenance and upkeep

Once you have your plot up and running than starts the task of maintaining it. Betty will guide you through seasonal tasks, crop rotation and tips on how to make your life easier!
16:00 - 16:30 Q&A with Betty Tordoff
16:30 Event closes
