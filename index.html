Please join us on Saturday 22nd May 2021 at The Landmark Arts Centre, Teddington. We are proudly holding our first ever event aimed at new and aspiring allotment holders. You may have recently taken on an allotment and be feeling overwhelmed, on the waiting list for an allotment or just interested in what would be involved. Come and hear from our speakers and gain insight, knowledge and tips on what's involved during your first year as an allotment holder.
