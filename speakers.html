Speakers

Mr Stuart Lodge

Stuart is a retired teacher and has held his allotment for 12 years. He has been the winner of 'Most Productive Category' for 6 of those 12 years and he attributes this to thorough planning.

Miss Sahera Chohan

Sahera is an established author and leadership coach who cares for her allotment as an escape from her working life. Having inherited her allotment 5 years ago after it had been neglected for some time, she understands the importance of good preperation from the very start.

Mr David Lillywhite

David took on a 10 rod plot 6 years ago with grand designs of having the entire plot producing crops within the first year. After pushing himself to the point of nearly giving up he realised the importance of starting small and enjoying it more! He has won 'Best Allotment' for the past 2 years.

Mrs Betty Tordoff

Betty has tended to her allotment together with her husband Jack for the last 22 years. Now retired, she brings a wealth of experience from her role as Head Gardener at Hampton Court Palace.
